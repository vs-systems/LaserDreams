An Architectural Presence

The Origen collection was born from the desire to create a chair that feels as though it has been carved from a single piece of wood. The joinery is entirely hidden, using traditional dovetail techniques that have been refined for a modern aesthetic. Each piece is numbered and signed by the master craftsman who oversaw its production in our Copenhagen atelier.
